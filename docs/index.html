East Greenwich.







The population of East Greenwich is about 15,000 and East Greenwich covers about 16.72 square miles. East Greenwich is one of the oldest towns in Rhode Island, having been established in 1677 after King Philip's war.


Some of my favorite places in East Greenwich include:

  • The yacht club
  • Felecias coffe
  • Fry Brook park.
